The Ottoman Hall

OUR PRODUCE IS FRESH, ORGANIC AND LOCAL.

Reminiscent of the reception space of a grand Ottoman palace,
the Ottoman Hall is our sumptuous dinner restaurant.

THE MAIN COURT

This peaceful, shaded courtyard leading off the reception area is the heart of Al Moudira, an informal outdoor living room where guests can enjoy breakfast, coffee, lunch, drinks and casual dinner, or just lounge in the shade while birds play around the bougainvillaea.

SHEHRAZAD

This is where Zeina most indulged her Arabian fantasies: the Shehrazad nightclub is inspired by Cairo’s wild and glamorous 1930s and is open exclusively for your private dinners or parties.

HOME FARM

Beyond the gardens of Al Moudira lies a small home farm with cows, a resident camel, donkeys, ducks, chickens as well as a vegetable garden that supplies a variety of vegetables and flowers for the table.

BESPOKE TRANSPORTS

Upon request, we could arrange for your airport transfers in our beautifiully restored vintage cars offering you a unique level of comfort and a nostalgic experience letting your mind wonder about stories dating back to the classic eras
